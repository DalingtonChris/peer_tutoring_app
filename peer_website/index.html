Features

Everything you need to learn faster

Built for students and peer tutors at university level — simple, fast, and effective.

🔍

Find Tutors Instantly

Browse tutors by subject or course name. Filter by category and view their full profile before reaching out.

💬

Real-Time Chat

Message tutors with live Socket.io-powered chat. See online status and get replies the moment they arrive.

🙋

Request Help

Post a help request for any course. Tutors see it on their dashboard and can reply directly to you.

⭐

Tutor Credit System

Tutors earn rating credits each time a new student starts a conversation with them. Track your impact.

📚

Subject Profiles

Tutors list every subject they teach with detailed descriptions so students know exactly what help is available.

🔔

Reply Notifications

Students see when their help request has been answered and can read the tutor's reply right in the app.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

Three simple steps to start getting or giving help.

1

Download & Create your account

Install the APK, register as a Student or Tutor, and you're in. No email verification, no waiting.

2

Find help or offer it

Students browse the tutor marketplace or post a request. Tutors set up their profile and list their subjects.

3

Connect & Learn

Message a tutor directly, get a reply to your request, or jump into a real-time chat session.
